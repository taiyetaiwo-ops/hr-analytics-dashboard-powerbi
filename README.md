# HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, compensation trends, and departmental performance.

The dashboard helps HR professionals and business leaders identify employee turnover patterns, understand workforce composition, and support data-driven workforce planning decisions.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Average Age
- Average Monthly Income

---

## Dashboard Features

### Workforce Analysis
- Employee distribution by gender
- Employee distribution by age group
- Workforce demographic insights

### Attrition Analysis
- Employee attrition by department
- Employee attrition by job role
- Attrition distribution by age group

### Compensation Analysis
- Average monthly income by job role
- Relationship between years at company and monthly income

### Interactive Functionality
- Dynamic filtering using slicers
- Interactive visualizations
- KPI monitoring and performance tracking

---

## Key Insights

- The overall attrition rate is 16.12%, indicating moderate employee turnover.
- Research & Development records the highest attrition among departments.
- Employees aged 25–34 represent the largest share of attrition cases.
- The workforce consists of approximately 60% male and 40% female employees.
- Managers and Research Directors earn the highest average monthly income.
- Employee tenure shows a weak relationship with income, suggesting other factors influence compensation.
